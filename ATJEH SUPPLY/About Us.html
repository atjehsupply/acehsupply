We Offer The Highest Quality Spices In The Market At A Very Competitive Price.

ATJEH SUPPLY was founded in 2020, based on Aceh province, Indonesia, is dedicated to becoming the leading spices and vegetable supplier of Aceh.

With this concept, ATJEH SUPPLY strives for continuous development and expansion. At present, the business scope mainly includes: Ginger, Turmeric, Cinnamon, Coffee, Black Pepper, Pear, and so on.
Atjeh supply is a company located in Indonesia engaged in agriculture. we are supplier of organic spices.
